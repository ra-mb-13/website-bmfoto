I'm Bárbara Morais and I live in the North of Portugal. I'm a woman, wife, mother, and physician. I'm self-taught photographer, and a lover of alternative photographic processes. I photograph for pleasure, as a form of self-knowledge and as a way to express my emotions. Occasionally, having availability, I also do some jobs that are asked of me. I exhibited my photographs at the Order of Physicians-Oporto and at the Eco Museu do Barroso. I won the first prize of photography of the Portuguese Association of General Clinic Physicians in 2015. In March of 2019, I had the honor of having collaborated and been interviewed by the magazine Spiral of Time, in its spring number, under the motto "green". In November 2021 I had the honor of being one of the guest artists who exhibited a work at the Zero Pixel Photography festival, which took place in Italy. In 2023 I had my first solo exhibitions, at the Order of Physicians - Porto and Order of Physicians - Regional Section of Vila Real. On the Flickr website since 2011, I has the invited artist by Contrasted gallery, in May 2023. My latest exhibition is now taking place at the Portuguese Center of Photography until June 1st! Come see what I do and leave your opinion!! Thank you all! For any contact you may need: bmbmmorais@gmail.com
